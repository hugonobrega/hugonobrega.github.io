Informal Amsterdam-Hamburg Set Theory Encounter

On Tuesday, 29 April, the set theory research groups of the Universities of Amsterdam and Hamburg are having an informal meeting to exchange ideas and discuss collaboration. This meeting involves four short presentations by the PhD students and postdocs from both places, and it takes place in the seminar room (F1.15) of Science Park 107

Everyone is cordially invited to join and listen.
